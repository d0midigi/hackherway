Hello, humanoids! I’m mindhackdiva (a.k.a. Yvonne Angelica in human form) of HackHerWay. My expertise lies in designing and implementing robust, high-performing internal and external network defenses that keep mission-critical systems safe and organizations operationally sound.

I’ve held senior roles, including ISSO, ISSE, Lead Cybersecurity Analyst, Vulnerability Management Specialist, and Network and Systems Security Engineer (NetSecOps), and my background combines rigorous technical training with years of real-world execution, giving me a deep understanding of both offensive and defensive strategies. I don’t just know the tools; I know how to apply them effectively in high-pressure, live environments.


With extensive experience in both adversary emulation and incident response, I bring a dual perspective to security. My years of hands-on training have been continuously validated by successful real-world deployments, allowing me to bridge the gap between theoretical vulnerabilities and practical, layered defense.


My journey began in the early '90s with a simple AOL Instant Messenger (AIM) chat, and it sparked a lifelong passion for defending digital infrastructure. Today, I focus on protecting organizations and the people behind them, enabling them to navigate a complex and often dangerous digital world safely while trying to making a meaningful difference.
